— Opening Note
GTA Web Works was started by Tim, a 17-year-old designer based in Vaughan, out of a simple frustration: local businesses across the GTA do incredible work and then hide behind websites that do them zero favors.
— The Story

We're new. We're not going to pretend otherwise. But being new has one big advantage: we're hungry, we care about every project like it's our first, and we price our work like a new studio should — fairly, transparently, and without the baggage of an agency overhead nobody asked for.

Every website we build is handmade. No page builders, no WordPress templates, no generic themes bought off a marketplace. We write the code ourselves, design the layouts ourselves, and make sure every pixel has a reason to be there. That sounds obvious, but most small business sites in the GTA are built by people who do none of those things.

Our whole model is built on a simple belief: a local business should own its website. You shouldn't be renting your online presence from a company that holds your content hostage if you stop paying. You shouldn't be on a monthly plan that bleeds you dry for basic hosting. You should pay once, own everything, and move on with your life.

We're based in Vaughan and proudly serve businesses across Richmond Hill, Newmarket, Markham, Toronto, and everywhere in between. If you've been putting off getting a website because you don't know where to start, or you've been quoted $5,000+ by an agency for something basic, we'd love to show you there's another way.

— Tim, Founder

What we
believe.

Four principles that shape every project we take on. They're not marketing copy. They're how we work.

— 01

You should own your site.

Your website is your storefront on the internet. You should own the domain, the files, the hosting account, and the content. No rentals. No dependencies. No hostage situations.

— 02

Prices should be transparent.

Our packages are listed on our site. No "contact us for a quote" games. No hidden fees. No surprise upsells. You know exactly what you're paying for before you ever send a message.

— 03

Design should be custom.

We don't use templates. Every site we build is designed from scratch for the specific business behind it. Your website should look like you, not like a thousand other businesses on the internet.

— 04

Speed is a feature.

A slow website loses customers before they even see it. We build with modern, lightweight code and host on fast platforms. Your site should load in under two seconds on every device.

— How we work

Rules of the studio.

— 01
No templates.
Every project starts with a blank canvas. We don't reuse layouts from past clients or buy themes off marketplaces. Your business deserves its own design.
— 02
No lock-ins.
You walk away with full ownership of your website, domain, and hosting. No monthly fees, no required maintenance contracts, no hostage situations.
— 03
No jargon.
We explain things in plain English. No "responsive paradigm" or "conversion funnel optimization." Just honest conversations about what you need and what we'll build.
— 04
No ghosting.
We respond to every message within 24 hours, usually within a few. You'll always know where your project stands and what happens next. That's the whole deal.
— 05
No compromises.
We'd rather take on fewer projects and do them well than churn out generic sites in volume. If a project isn't the right fit, we'll say so. Honestly.
